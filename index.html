I'm a graduate (master) student of Mathematics and Computer Science at Darmstadt University of Technology.
Previously, I did my bachelor in Mathematics and Computer Science at Darmstadt with research and exchange stays at The University of Oslo and The University of Tokyo.
I am also working part time as a software developer at Seltmann Webdesign GmbH.

Research Interests

I mainly work in Computable Analysis under supervision of Prof. Dr. Martin Ziegler.
I focus on practical implementations of algorithms for validated numerics, using Norbert Müller's iRRAM Framework.
I am also interested in algorithmics, machine learning and applications of mathematics in computer science in a broader sense.
