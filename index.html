Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my homepage.
I am a Ph.D. student at The University of Tokyo, Graduate School of Arts and Sciences.
My supervisor is Akitoshi Kawamura.
Previously, I did my bachelor in Mathematics and Computer Science as well as my master in Mathematics at Darmstadt University of Technology, supervised by Martin Ziegler. During my undergraduate and graduate education I have also spent some time at The University of Oslo and The University of Tokyo.
